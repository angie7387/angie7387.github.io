A White Handkerchief

Hold a white gauze in hand, the towel is fragrant, and give it to your lover;
Two mandarin ducks are embroidered on the towel, and they form a pair and carry it by your side.

You are going to go far, the road is long, don't forget the towel;
Wearing a white gauze next to him, he glanced at it as if he was seeing me.

I hope my lover goes to a distant place, returns to his hometown early, and returns to the old gate wall;
We are also like mandarin ducks, in a pair, as if on a veil.

Send you a white gauze, the scarf is fragrant, and carry it by your side;
Don't forget the towel giver, go home early, don't teach me to think day and night.
