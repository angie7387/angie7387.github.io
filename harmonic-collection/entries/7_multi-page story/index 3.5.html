I Wanna Be a Cowboy’s Sweetheart

I am willing to marry a cowherd from the neighboring village,
I love him for being affectionate and healthy.
Every day, maddened and madly sing together,
Sooner or later we will be a pair.

I am willing to marry a cowherd from the neighboring village,
Play with him on the ranch.
He played the flute for me to admire,
I sing for him.
